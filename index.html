Meet Atlas

What is the FIRST Robotics Competition?

The FIRST Robotics Competition is a national high-school level event held every year, where teams of students come together to compete with their custom built robots in a unique game, which is different for each season. Teams are competing with and against each other, showing off technical skills used in the field of robotics, including but not limited to, mecahnical design, electrical engineering, programming, graphic design, social marketing and strategy.

Who are We?

We are The NASA Knights, FRC team 122, a community-based robotics team in the Hampton Roads area in Viriginia. The team operates out of the New Horizons Regional Educational Center in Newport News. The school houses our team's shop and robots both in and out of season.

Mechanical Design

After we learn the rules of the game and possible strategies we can employ, we begin our design process. We start by looking at any robot constraints we have and then brainstorm ideas for different mechanisms on the robot. We then narrow our choices and test concepts we are interested in. As we do this, we evaluate product architecture and industrial design by building for modularity and with our drive team in mind. Then we prototype our ideas and improve upon them.
